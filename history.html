2014-2015: Cascade Effect

For our second year together, we knew exactly what we would need to do in order to be as successful as our rookie year. Now a group of friends who work exceptionally well together, we built another fast and maneuverable but simple robot, this time named “X.” We made it back to the World Championship, and became the first team from Texas to receive an award- the Design Award!

2013-2014: Block Party

While the team QuadX had been in existence for a year already, it was essentially our rookie year, since everyone was new to the team- we had 5 rookies, and nobody had ever been a member of the Hardware team. Through hard work, creativity, and sheer will, however, we made it to the World Championship! We had never expected to have one of the best-performing robots in the world at the beginning the year, and making it to the finals at Worlds was a fantastic conclusion to our first year together.
