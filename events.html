STEM Advocacy Conference of Texas

SACOT is an organization founded and run by FIRST teams to increase STEM. SACOT is an organization founded and run by FIRST teams geared towards spreading awareness and accessibility of STEM in Texas public schools.

Interested in being a key player in advocating for STEM to members of Texas Legislature? Get involved!
Any Texas team can become a member of SACOT and make a difference in their schools. It's great for the community, your resume, any your leadership skills! Go to sacot.org and sign up today.

On January 21-23, Texas Legislature will be in session. Teams that attend will:

  1. Educate state, local, and school governments about the importance of STEM
  2. Give a voice to underserved communities
  3. Advocate for legislation to spread STEM
  4. Organize and represent the opinions of high school students about STEM
