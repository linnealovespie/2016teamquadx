STEM Advocacy Conference of Texas

SACOT is an organization founded and run by FIRST teams to increase STEM. SACOT is an organization founded and run by FIRST teams geared towards spreading awareness and accessibility of STEM in Texas public schools.

Interested in being a key player in advocating for STEM to members of Texas Legislature? Get involved!
Any Texas team can become a member of SACOT and make a difference in their schools. It's great for the community, your resume, any your leadership skills! Go to sacot.org and sign up today.

On January 21-23, Texas Legislature will be in session. Teams that attend will:

  1. Educate state, local, and school governments about the importance of STEM
  2. Give a voice to underserved communities
  3. Advocate for legislation to spread STEM
  4. Organize and represent the opinions of high school students about STEM

Other Outreach Initiatives

Workshops

We've hosted workshops on topics from CAD to Marketing. You can find the accompanying material on our resources page.

FTC Collaboration

Along with our workshops, we've worked with local FTC teams to improve our sensor usage, robot designs, and match performance.

STEM Fairs

From Mini Maker Faires to South By Southwest Interact, we spread awareness of ViperBots and FIRST at local STEM fairs.

Central Texas Kickoff

This year, we organized Kickoff at Vandegrift Higschool. Our members organized and ran team building activities and workshops.

Lego Drive

We collected over 200 sets of Lego for children living in public housing to give them exposure to STEM they wouldn't have otherwise.

Local Business Tours

We reach out to local businesses, such as QSystems, a company building 747 simulators, to explore applications of our robotics skills.
