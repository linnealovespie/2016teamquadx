Meet Beatriz, our Foreign Exchange Student

Beatriz Passanezi joined us this season as a foreign exchange student from Sao Paulo, Brazil. Our engineering teacher told her about ViperBots at the beginning of the school year, and she's been on the team since kickoff. She does a lot of work with the hardware team, learning right along with the rest of the rookies. She'll be at Vandegrift until January, so she will get to experience the entire build process, and even a couple qualifiers. When she heads back to Brazil, our goal is to set up FTC and other FIRST teams in Sao Paulo!
